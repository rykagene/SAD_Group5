BULACAN STATE UNIVERSITY

Library

The Bulacan State University Library, through its resources, facility, and staff, is
dedicated to providing open access to information and to offering the services
and tools with which to locate and interpret that information. As patrons’ needs
and information technologies continue to evolve, so will the means with which
the Library attempts to fulfill its role within the community.
